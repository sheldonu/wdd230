Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide more commercial attention to the company and lure more customers to try their product. It will also be the place where customers can find exactly what they want and how they are able to order foods they would like to try.

Audience

Anyone who is living in Carlsbad, or between Los Angeles and San Diego. It is targeted to any person of any age, especially those who love organic foods.
